Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Kobamelo College programs, admissions, and more.

Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, you'll need:

  • Completed application form
  • Certified copy of your ID
  • Latest academic results or certificates
  • Application fee (if applicable)

Some programs may have additional requirements. Please check the specific program page or contact us for details.

Course fees vary depending on the program and duration. Our fees are competitive and we offer flexible payment plans. For specific pricing information:

  • Visit our Courses page for program-specific fees
  • Contact our admissions office
  • Attend one of our open days

We also offer discounts for early registration and group enrollments.

Yes, Kobamelo College is proud to be accredited by several recognized bodies:

  • QCTO (Quality Council for Trades and Occupations)
  • SAIOSH (South African Institute of Occupational Safety and Health)
  • CPD Group
  • Step Ahead CPD

Our accreditation ensures that our programs meet national standards for quality education.

We provide comprehensive career support to help our graduates succeed:

  • Career counseling and guidance
  • Resume writing workshops
  • Interview preparation
  • Job placement assistance
  • Alumni network

Many of our graduates have found employment through our industry connections and career services.

We offer flexible study options to accommodate different schedules:

  • Full-time classroom-based programs
  • Part-time evening classes for working professionals
  • Blended learning (combination of online and in-person)
  • Distance learning options for certain programs

Please check with our admissions office for the study options available for your chosen program.

We offer several options to help make education more accessible:

  • Payment plans that allow you to pay in installments
  • Bursaries for qualifying students
  • Discounts for early registration
  • Group enrollment discounts

To apply for financial assistance, please contact our finance office with proof of income and any supporting documents.
