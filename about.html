About Kobamelo College

Our Mission

At Kobamelo College, we strive to create a supportive and stimulating learning environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, and critical thinking.

  • Provide accessible and affordable education programs
  • Foster a culture of excellence, innovation, and entrepreneurship
  • Develop partnerships to promote economic growth
  • Empower students to become active citizens and leaders

Our History

2023: Establishment

Kobamelo College was founded with a vision to provide quality education and skills training to the community.

2024: First Accreditation

Received accreditation from QCTO and SAIOSH, marking a significant milestone in our commitment to quality education.

2025: Expansion

Expanded our course offerings and saw a 20% increase in student enrollment, demonstrating growing trust in our institution.
